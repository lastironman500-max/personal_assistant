# Findings

## Strategy Analysis (from 100-to-10k-5day-strategy.md)

### Best Path: AI Service Arbitrage (Priority 9/10)
- Sell landing pages, copywriting, pitch decks to businesses
- Fulfill with AI in 2-4 hours per deliverable
- Price: $500-$2,000 per project
- Margin: 90%+ (AI does 80% of work)
- Bottleneck: Client acquisition, not delivery

### Secondary: AI Freelancing on Platforms (Priority 8/10)
- AI chatbot setup: $200-$1,000
- AI workflow automation: $300-$2,000
- AI prompt engineering: $50-$500
- Fastest platform: Upwork (aggressive bidding)

### Probability to hit $1,000 in 5 days
- Service arbitrage + freelancing: 15-25% probability
- Needs 1-2 clients at $500+ each
- Or 3-5 smaller gigs at $200-300

## Agent Architecture Findings

### Token Efficiency Strategy
- CEO agent (opus): planning only, minimal calls
- Outreach agent (haiku): bulk message generation
- Fulfillment agent (sonnet): quality deliverables
- Skill creator (haiku): template extraction

### Reusable Skills to Create
1. `cold-outreach-generator` — generates personalized cold DMs
2. `landing-page-builder` — creates landing page copy + structure
3. `pitch-deck-writer` — generates pitch deck content
4. `upwork-proposal-writer` — writes winning Upwork bids
5. `portfolio-builder` — creates sample work for showcasing
6. `invoice-generator` — creates professional invoices

## Platform Research
- Upwork: First job 3-7 days for aggressive bidders, $25-150/hr
- Fiverr: Promoted gigs ($20) accelerate visibility
- LinkedIn: 1-3% cold outreach conversion, highest ticket
- Carrd: Free portfolio site, sufficient for credibility
