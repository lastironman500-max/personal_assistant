## What We Think Your Daily Workflow Looks Like

1. **Check messages across 5 apps** (Messenger, IG, iMessage, Email, CRM)
2. **Reply to prospects** on whichever platform they messaged you
3. **Send outbound messages** to prospects (target list already built by another tool)
4. **Cold call block** — prep, dial, leave voicemails, log outcomes
5. **Follow up** with prospects who haven't replied
6. **Update CRM** with notes, deal stages, activity logs
