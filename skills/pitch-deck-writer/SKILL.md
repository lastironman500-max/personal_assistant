# Pitch Deck Writer

Create investor-ready or sales pitch decks. 10-12 slides following proven frameworks.

## Inputs Required
- **Company/Product**: Name and one-liner
- **Audience**: Investors, clients, or partners
- **Problem**: What pain exists
- **Ask**: What you want (funding, partnership, sale)

## Slide Framework (10 slides)

| Slide | Content | Time |
|-------|---------|------|
| 1. Title | Company name, tagline, presenter | 10s |
| 2. Problem | The pain, who has it, how big | 30s |
| 3. Solution | Your product, how it solves the pain | 45s |
| 4. Demo/Product | Screenshots, flow, key features | 60s |
| 5. Market | TAM/SAM/SOM, market trends | 30s |
| 6. Business Model | How you make money, pricing | 30s |
| 7. Traction | Metrics, growth, customers | 45s |
| 8. Competition | Positioning matrix, differentiation | 30s |
| 9. Team | Key people, relevant experience | 20s |
| 10. Ask | What you need, use of funds, timeline | 30s |

## Rules
1. One idea per slide. No walls of text.
2. Headlines tell the story — someone skimming only headlines should get the pitch.
3. Use concrete numbers over adjectives.
4. Problem slide should make the audience nod.
5. Competition slide: never say "no competition."

## Deliverables
1. **Slide-by-slide copy**: All text, speaker notes
2. **Visual direction**: What to show on each slide
3. **One-line story arc**: The narrative thread
