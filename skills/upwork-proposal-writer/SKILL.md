# Upwork Proposal Writer

Write winning Upwork proposals that stand out from generic bids. Optimized for new sellers with no reviews.

## Inputs Required
- **Job posting**: The full Upwork job description
- **Your service**: What you deliver
- **Turnaround time**: How fast you can deliver
- **Price**: Your bid amount
- **Portfolio link**: Sample work (optional)

## Proposal Framework (200 words max)

```
Paragraph 1 — Hook (2 sentences)
  Restate their specific problem. Show you READ the posting.

Paragraph 2 — Solution (3 sentences)
  What you'll deliver, how, and by when. Be concrete.

Paragraph 3 — Proof (2 sentences)
  Sample, case study, or free offer to reduce risk.

Paragraph 4 — CTA (1 sentence)
  Ask a clarifying question to start dialogue.
```

## Rules
1. First sentence must reference something SPECIFIC from their job post.
2. Never start with "I am a..." or "I have X years experience..."
3. Include a deliverable timeline (e.g., "48-hour turnaround").
4. For new accounts: offer a small free sample to overcome zero-review barrier.
5. Bid within 5 minutes of job posting for maximum visibility.
6. Keep proposals under 200 words. Clients skim.

## New Seller Strategy
- Bid 20% below market rate for first 3 jobs
- Offer "first milestone free" to build reviews
- Apply to jobs posted < 1 hour ago
- Target jobs with < 10 proposals
- Use Upwork's "boosted" proposal feature ($5-15 per boost)

## Example Output

**Job**: "Need someone to write landing page copy for my SaaS product"

> You need landing page copy that converts visitors into trial signups — not just words that sound nice.
>
> I'll write your full landing page (hero, features, social proof, CTA sections) optimized for conversion. You'll get 2 variants to A/B test plus a headline swipe file. Delivered in 48 hours.
>
> Here's a sample landing page I wrote last week: [link]. Happy to do your hero section as a free sample so you can judge quality before committing.
>
> Quick question — do you have existing customer testimonials I can weave in, or should I draft placeholder social proof?
