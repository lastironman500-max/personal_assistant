# Landing Page Builder

Create high-converting landing page copy and structure for SaaS, agencies, and small businesses. This is the primary fulfillment skill for AI service arbitrage.

## Inputs Required
- **Business name**: Company or product name
- **What it does**: One-sentence description
- **Target customer**: Who buys this
- **Key benefit**: Primary value proposition
- **Existing content**: Any current site URL, brand guidelines, testimonials (optional)

## Landing Page Structure

### Section 1: Hero
- **Headline**: Clear benefit statement (max 10 words)
- **Subheadline**: How it works in one sentence
- **CTA button**: Action-oriented (e.g., "Start Free Trial", "Get Your Demo")
- **Social proof snippet**: "Trusted by 500+ companies" or logos

### Section 2: Problem
- 3 pain points the customer faces
- Use their language, not jargon

### Section 3: Solution
- How the product solves each pain point
- Feature → Benefit mapping (3-4 items)

### Section 4: How It Works
- 3-step process (simple, visual)
- Step 1: [Action] → Step 2: [Action] → Step 3: [Result]

### Section 5: Social Proof
- 2-3 testimonials (real or template placeholders)
- Metrics: "X% improvement", "Y hours saved"
- Company logos if available

### Section 6: Pricing (optional)
- 2-3 tier pricing table
- Highlight recommended tier
- Annual vs monthly toggle

### Section 7: FAQ
- 5-6 common objections addressed
- Reduce friction for conversion

### Section 8: Final CTA
- Repeat headline + CTA button
- Risk reversal: "30-day money-back guarantee"

## Deliverables
1. **Copy document**: All sections with final copy
2. **Wireframe description**: Section layout guide for designer/developer
3. **SEO meta**: Title tag, meta description, OG tags
4. **2 headline variants**: For A/B testing

## Quality Checklist
- [ ] Headline passes the "5-second test" (clear on first read)
- [ ] Every feature maps to a benefit
- [ ] CTA appears at least 3 times on page
- [ ] Social proof is specific, not vague
- [ ] No jargon — written at 8th grade reading level
- [ ] Mobile-friendly structure (no wide tables)
