# Cold Outreach Generator

Generate personalized cold outreach messages for LinkedIn, email, and Twitter/X to sell AI-powered services.

## Inputs Required
- **Service offered**: What you're selling (landing page, copywriting, pitch deck, automation)
- **Target audience**: Who you're reaching (startup founders, agency owners, SMB owners)
- **Platform**: LinkedIn DM, email, or Twitter/X reply
- **Price range**: Your rate for the service
- **Portfolio link**: URL to your work samples (optional)

## Message Framework

### LinkedIn DM (150 words max)
```
Line 1: Personalized hook (reference their company/post/role)
Line 2: Pain point acknowledgment
Line 3: What you do + specific result
Line 4: Social proof or sample offer
Line 5: Low-friction CTA (no "hop on a call")
```

### Email (200 words max)
```
Subject: [Specific result] for [Company Name]
Line 1: Personalized observation about their business
Line 2-3: Problem you noticed (outdated site, weak copy, no landing page)
Line 4-5: Your offer + specific deliverable + timeline
Line 6: Free sample or audit offer
Line 7: Simple CTA
```

### Twitter/X Reply (280 chars)
```
Agree/add value to their tweet → bridge to your service → link or DM offer
```

## Rules
1. Never be generic. Reference something specific about the prospect.
2. Lead with value, not features.
3. CTA must be low-friction: "Want me to send a free mockup?" not "Let's schedule a call."
4. No more than 1 follow-up per prospect per week.
5. Track response rates per template variant.

## Volume Targets
- Day 1: 50 LinkedIn DMs + 20 emails
- Day 2: 50 LinkedIn DMs + 20 emails + 10 Twitter replies
- Day 3-5: Follow up on openers + new outreach

## Conversion Expectations
- LinkedIn DM: 3-5% reply rate, 1% conversion to paid
- Email: 5-10% open rate, 1-2% reply rate
- Twitter: Variable, best for warm leads

## Example Output

### LinkedIn DM — Selling Landing Page Service
> Hey [Name] — saw your launch post about [Product]. Congrats!
>
> Quick thought: your current site doesn't have a dedicated landing page for [specific feature]. I build high-converting landing pages for SaaS companies using AI — typically delivered in 48 hours for $500-800.
>
> Here's one I did recently: [portfolio link]
>
> Want me to mock up a version for [Product]? Free, no strings — takes me 30 min.
