### 1. Review Monitor & Responder (Score: 37)
The highest-scoring idea because demand is undeniable (every business with a Google listing has this pain), the response loop is fully automatable via platform APIs, and the value is immediately visible. First revenue within days via cold outreach to restaurants or dental practices.

### 2. Automated Bookkeeping Categorizer (Score: 36)
Freelancers and solopreneurs universally hate this task and pay monthly for Wave/Bench/Pilot alternatives. An AI-native version undercuts incumbents on price while matching accuracy. Plaid integration is cheap and fast.

### 3. Domain & SSL Expiry Guardian (Score: 35)
Zero-to-deploy in a weekend. WHOIS and SSL Labs APIs are free. Pain is universal and recurring. Agencies managing 50+ client domains are a particularly high-value customer segment with low churn.

### 4. Competitor Price Intelligence Feed (Score: 35)
E-commerce operators already pay $200–$1,000/month for tools like Prisync and Wiser. A GPT-native version that writes strategic commentary instead of just showing raw price tables creates a clear differentiation story.

### 5. SaaS Spend Tracker & Optimizer (Score: 35)
Average company wastes 30% of SaaS budget on unused tools (source: Zylo research). AI can detect this from transaction data alone. Customer success practically sells itself — the tool pays for itself in month one.
