## FAQ

Questions? We've got answers.

---

**Q: How long does setup actually take?**
A: Most teams are up and running in under 10 minutes. If you use Jira, Linear, Mixpanel, or Amplitude, we have one-click connectors. Custom integrations via API typically take 30–60 minutes.

---

**Q: Do I need an engineer to set this up?**
A: No. FlowMetrics is built for PMs. The full setup — connecting tools, configuring dashboards, inviting your team — requires zero technical knowledge.

---

**Q: What tools does FlowMetrics integrate with?**
A: We integrate natively with Jira, Linear, Mixpanel, Amplitude, Segment, Heap, Google Analytics 4, Intercom, Zendesk, and Slack. Full API access is available on Growth and Enterprise plans.

---

**Q: How is FlowMetrics different from Mixpanel or Amplitude?**
A: Mixpanel and Amplitude are excellent event analytics tools. FlowMetrics isn't a replacement — it's a layer on top. We pull in data from your analytics tools *and* your project management tools to connect product output (features shipped) with user outcomes (what happened next). That cross-tool synthesis is what's unique.

---

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes. FlowMetrics is SOC 2 Type II certified (Enterprise) and uses AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit. We never sell or share your data. See our full security policy at flowmetrics.io/security.

---

**Q: What happens after my free trial ends?**
A: You'll be prompted to choose a plan. If you don't upgrade, your account moves to the Starter (free) plan automatically. Your data is preserved — no panic, no pressure.
