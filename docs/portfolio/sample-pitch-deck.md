## SLIDE 2 — THE PROBLEM

**Headline:** Customer support is broken at scale — and getting worse.

**Three core pains:**

**1. Volume is exploding.**
The average SaaS company receives 3x more support tickets today than in 2020. Headcount hasn't kept up.

**2. Response times are killing retention.**
67% of customers say slow support is the primary reason they churn. The average first-response time across mid-market SaaS is 14 hours.

**3. Human agents are handling work a machine should do.**
72% of all support tickets fall into just 8 repeatable categories: billing, password reset, cancellation, status inquiry, onboarding help, refund request, account access, and basic how-to questions.

**Punchline:**
Companies are spending $180,000/year per support agent to answer questions that never needed a human in the first place.

## SLIDE 3 — THE SOLUTION

**Headline:** AutoReply AI: The first support platform that resolves tickets, not just routes them.

**What we do:**
AutoReply AI integrates with your existing helpdesk (Zendesk, Intercom, Freshdesk) and trains on your documentation, past tickets, and product knowledge base. Within 48 hours, it handles inbound tickets end-to-end — no templates, no decision trees, no human needed for the 80% of issues it already knows how to solve.

**Key differentiators:**

- **Resolution, not deflection.** We don't send users to a help article. We solve the problem, take the action, close the ticket.
- **Learns from your team.** Every ticket a human agent closes becomes a training signal. AutoReply gets smarter every week.
- **Seamless escalation.** When a ticket needs a human, AutoReply hands off with full context — no user has to repeat themselves.

**One-line pitch:**
AutoReply AI is Zendesk's AI co-pilot that works 24/7, never calls in sick, and gets better every single day.

### A1 — Customer Case Study: Trellis

**Challenge:** Trellis (Series A, 95 employees) was handling 4,200 support tickets/month with a 3-person support team. Average first-response time: 11 hours. CSAT: 3.9/5.

**AutoReply Implementation:**

- Configured to handle billing, access, and onboarding ticket categories

**Results (after 90 days):**
- 84% auto-resolve rate
- Average resolution time: 71 seconds (down from 11 hours)
- CSAT improved to 4.7/5
- Support headcount reduced from 3 to 1 FTE through natural attrition
- Estimated annual savings: $420,000

---

### A2 — Technical Architecture (Summary)

**Ingestion Layer:** Real-time webhook integration with Zendesk, Intercom, Freshdesk APIs

**Classification Engine:** Fine-tuned LLM classifier trained on 50M+ historical support tickets to identify intent, urgency, and required action

**Resolution Engine:** LLM-based response generator grounded in customer-specific documentation, past resolutions, and live API calls to customer systems (billing, auth, CRM)

**Action Layer:** Secure API connectors to Stripe, Auth0, Salesforce, Shopify, and custom endpoints — enabling AutoReply to take real actions, not just draft responses

**Human Escalation:** Confidence-scored escalation with full conversation context, classification tags, and suggested next steps passed to the human agent queue

**Security:** SOC 2 Type I certified. All customer data isolated in tenant-specific vector stores. PII handling compliant with GDPR and CCPA.
