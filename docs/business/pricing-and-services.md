## Process

We follow a simple 4-step workflow to ensure your satisfaction:

### 1. Discovery
Schedule a 20–30 minute call to discuss your goals, audience, brand voice, and any specific requirements. We'll ask clarifying questions and set expectations for delivery.

### 2. Draft
We research your industry, competitors, and audience, then craft initial copy tailored to your needs. You'll receive a complete draft for review.

### 3. Revision
We incorporate your feedback and make revisions. Up to **2 rounds of revisions** included in all packages. Additional revisions available at $100/hour.

### 4. Delivery
Final copy delivered in your preferred format (Word, Google Doc, Notion, or directly in your CMS). You're ready to launch.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How quickly can you turn around a project?
Most landing page and pitch deck projects complete in 48 hours. Full website copy typically takes 3–5 business days depending on scope. Rush delivery available—contact us for details.

### How many revisions are included?
All projects include up to 2 rounds of revisions at no additional cost. Each round allows you to request refinements, adjustments, or strategic changes. Additional revisions billed at $100/hour.

### What's your refund policy?
If you're not satisfied with the work, we offer one free revision round to address concerns. If expectations aren't met after revision, we'll provide a 50% refund of fees paid. We stand behind our work and aim to make you happy.

### Can you work with tight deadlines?
Yes, we prioritize rush projects when our schedule allows. Rush fees apply: add 25% to the project price for next-day delivery. Contact us to confirm availability.

### Do you provide revisions after delivery?
Post-delivery revisions (beyond the included 2 rounds) are billed at $100/hour. We recommend scheduling all feedback within the revision window to maximize value.
