## Research Notes

- All 5 businesses verified via direct website URLs, LinkedIn, BBB, or press coverage.
- NY and FL represent the two highest-volume states for ADA Title III website lawsuits.
- Fashion/apparel accounts for approximately 35% of all ADA ecommerce lawsuits.
- All 5 are independent/boutique operations unlikely to have in-house accessibility teams.
- Saved by the Dress and Marcella NYC have the highest web traffic and are the highest-priority targets.
