## Search Methodology

For each company, the following searches were performed:

1. `"[company name]" "[domain]" email [owner name]`
2. `[owner name] email [domain] contact`
3. `site:linkedin.com "[owner name]" "[company name]"`
4. Direct website fetch of contact pages
5. Cross-reference with ZoomInfo, RocketReach, ContactOut, Salesgear.io, Wiza

### Email Pattern Guessing Logic

Where no direct email was found, patterns tested (in priority order):
- `firstname@domain.com`
- `first.last@domain.com`
- `info@domain.com`
- `hello@domain.com`
- `contact@domain.com`
- `smile@domain.com` (dental/wellness)
