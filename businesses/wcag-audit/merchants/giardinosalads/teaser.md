### Top 3 Issues Found (Preview)

**Issue 1: Invisible "Order Now" Buttons**
- What's wrong: The "Order Now" and "Order Catering" call-to-action buttons have a text-to-background contrast ratio of 1:1 — meaning the button text is effectively invisible to anyone with low vision or color-perception differences.
- Where: Primary ordering buttons across all 9 location slides in the hero carousel
- Why it matters: Customers with low vision or color blindness cannot read these buttons, directly blocking them from placing orders online. With 11 locations and online ordering as a key revenue channel, this affects a meaningful share of your customer base.

**Issue 2: Duplicate Page IDs Breaking Screen Readers**
- What's wrong: Multiple elements on the page share the same HTML id value ("ordering"), which causes screen reader software to malfunction — it either skips elements entirely or reads the wrong one.
- Where: "Order Now" and "Order Catering" navigation buttons
- Why it matters: A customer using a screen reader (common among blind and low-vision users) may be unable to find or activate your online ordering links at all.

**Issue 3: Form Field With No Label (reCAPTCHA)**
- What's wrong: A form field embedded in the page has no accessible label — screen readers announce it as an unnamed, unknown input, leaving users unable to interact with it correctly.
- Where: Contact/order form reCAPTCHA field
- Why it matters: Screen reader users cannot complete form submissions, cutting off an entire channel for catering inquiries and orders.

### What This Means for Giardino Gourmet Salads
With 11 locations across Miami and a busy online ordering platform, accessibility barriers on your homepage directly translate to lost orders from customers with disabilities — a population representing roughly 1 in 4 adults in the US. Beyond lost revenue, restaurants operating at this scale face increasing scrutiny under ADA Title III, with demand letters and litigation targeting businesses whose websites prevent equal access. The issues found are concrete and fixable.
