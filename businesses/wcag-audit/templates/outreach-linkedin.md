## LinkedIn Outreach Rules

1. **Daily limits:** 15-20 connection requests/day (stay under 80/week)
2. **Message limits:** 100-150 messages/week to existing connections
3. **Never send the scan results in the connection request** — wait for acceptance
4. **Always personalize** with their company name, industry, and specific findings
5. **Include a screenshot** in at least one follow-up (screenshots in DMs are highly engaging)
6. **Best sending times:** Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10 AM in the prospect's timezone
7. **Stop sequence** after 2 follow-ups with no response — do not spam
8. **Profile optimization:** Update your headline to include "WCAG Accessibility" and "ADA Compliance"
