## FAQ

**Q: Is this a full manual accessibility audit?**

This is an automated scan using axe-core (the industry-standard engine used by Google, Microsoft, and the BBC), enhanced with AI-powered analysis. It detects approximately 57% of WCAG issues — every programmatically-detectable violation. The report identifies what requires additional manual testing and provides a clear next-steps recommendation. For most businesses that have never done any accessibility testing, this covers the critical first 57% at a fraction of the cost of a manual audit.

**Q: Will this make my website ADA compliant?**

No scan or audit — automated or manual — guarantees compliance. What this report does is identify your highest-risk accessibility barriers so you can fix them proactively, significantly reducing your legal exposure. The remediation roadmap tells your developer exactly what to fix and in what order.

**Q: Why not just use a free tool like WAVE?**

Free tools produce raw technical output that requires developer expertise to interpret. Our report translates findings into plain English a business owner can understand, prioritizes by legal risk, includes code-level fix instructions, and delivers a professional document you can forward to your lawyer, board, or web developer. The difference is the narration layer — data vs. actionable intelligence.

**Q: What happens after I order?**

1. You send us your website URL
2. Our system scans your site within 24 hours
3. AI analyzes and prioritizes all findings
4. You receive a professional PDF report via email
5. (Compliance tier) We schedule your monthly re-scans automatically

**Q: Can you fix the issues you find?**

Our core service is identification and remediation guidance. The report includes developer-ready fix instructions with before/after code examples. If you need implementation help, we can connect you with trusted accessibility developers.

**Q: Do you offer refunds?**

Yes. If we scan your site and find zero violations (extremely rare — 94.8% of websites have detectable issues), we'll refund your payment in full. If you're unsatisfied with the report quality, contact us within 7 days for a full refund.

**Q: Is my data secure?**

We scan only publicly accessible pages — the same content any visitor sees in their browser. We do not access password-protected areas, store customer data, or share findings with anyone other than you.

**Q: How is this different from an accessibility overlay widget?**

Overlay widgets (like accessiBe, UserWay) add a cosmetic layer on top of your website without fixing the underlying code. The FTC fined accessiBe $1 million in January 2025 for false compliance claims. 22.6% of ADA web lawsuits in H1 2025 targeted sites with overlays installed. Our scan identifies the actual code-level issues so you can fix them properly.
